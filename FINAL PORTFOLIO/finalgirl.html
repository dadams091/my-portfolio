Final Girl

    Welcome to Joyland! A place where happiness thrives! Originally opened in 1958, Joyland has been a place where families have come to re-experience the nostalgia and exhilaration of childhood! Meet our workers: James, Katya, Crystal & Michael. After an exhausting day of plastic smiles and weariness, closing shop feels unusually eerie tonight. Then again, this could be because a psycho killer happens to be hiding in one of the park’s buildings and happens to have cut power to all the exits. Can they find the parts to revive the park’s power and make it out before the killer catches up to them?
