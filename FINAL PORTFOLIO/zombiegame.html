Zombie Game

About: Zombie game is a top-down arcade style shooter. You and your family are being hunted by an endless herd of zombies! Use your special ops experience to eliminate as many threats as you can before the threats eliminate you and your family. -Designed by Dakota Adams










Features include: Random power up drops (infinite ammo, protective shield, and quickness) that spawn on kill, Ammo count - 8 bullets before reload, high score counter ("High Kills"), 3 lives - loss of life when zombie passes player threshold
