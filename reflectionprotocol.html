Reflection Protocol


About

Game Synopsis: "Reflection Protocol" is a first-person 3D puzzle-solving platformer that follows the adventure of a curious teenage child who accidentally finds herself lost in time. Her father, who previously worked as a scientist in a government-funded laboratory, left his job to conduct personal research from the bunker located in his backyard. While exploring her father's bunker, the child takes cover inside one of his machines upon hearing her father's footsteps. As a result, she accidentally time-travels to arrive at a future date and within her father's laboratory. The game's primary mechanics center around using physics and her slingshot to solve puzzles and progress through the storyline. Players can discover and use different ammo types such as stones, gravity, and shootable-cameras in innovative ways. To return home, the protagonist must utilize their problem-solving skills and trusted slingshot to piece time back together and return to her worried father.
-Designed by SpaceKnot
